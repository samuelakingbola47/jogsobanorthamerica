Our Story

About JOGSOBA
North America

JOGSOBA North America, Inc. is a non-profit old students' organization whose founding purpose and continuing mission is to reach out to and be the platform for social interaction and an economic conduit for all Old Students of Ijebu-Ode Grammar School in the Diaspora, especially in North America and The Caribbean.

We are bound by a common thread — the formative years spent within the storied walls of Ijebu-Ode Grammar School. Our association preserves that bond across borders, generations, and decades, channeling it into purposeful service to our Alma Mater and the communities she shaped.

1913
School Founded

Ijebu-Ode Grammar School was founded on January 20, 1913 by the Church Missionary Society (C.M.S. / Anglican Mission). Located along Abeokuta Road in the Obalende area of Ijebu-Ode, Ogun State, Nigeria, the school stands as a beacon of secondary education in West Africa — having shaped generations of leaders, professionals, and servants of society for over 110 years.


"Dawn of life, The source of Light, and Beacon of Secondary Education"

Join Us

Join the Brotherhood

Every Old Boy of Ijebu-Ode Grammar School in North America, Canada, or the Caribbean has a home here. Our association is built on values that JOGS instilled in us.

JOGSOBA North America unites alumni from across the diaspora in fellowship, purposeful service, and shared investment in the future of Ijebu-Ode Grammar School. Membership is open to all verified Old Boys residing in North America and the Caribbean.

Making a Difference at Home

We don't just remember our Alma Mater — we invest in it.

Featured Initiative

Solar Power Donation — April 2021

JOGSOBA North America proudly donated a complete solar power system — including panels, inverters, batteries, and installation equipment — to the Administrative Building and Science Laboratories of Ijebu-Ode Grammar School. This initiative ensures uninterrupted power supply for administrative operations and scientific learning, directly benefiting hundreds of current students.

🎓 Centenary Celebrations — January 2013

JOGSOBA North America was proud to participate in the school's Centenary Celebrations marking 100 years of educational excellence. Members traveled from across North America to join Old Boys worldwide in honoring this historic milestone at Ijebu-Ode Grammar School.
